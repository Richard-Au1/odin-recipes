German Potato Salad

Here is the recipe of german potato salad from all recipes website. It has more of sour taste than amaerican poatao salad because vinegar is used as a replacement to mayonnaise.

Ingredients

    Potatoes
    Bacon
    Onion
    Vinegar
    Sugar
    Seasonings and herbs

Steps

    1. Boil, drain, and cool the diced and peeld potatoes.
    2. Fry the bacon in a skillet, then cook the onion in the bacon grease
    3. Add the vingar, sugar, water, salt, and pepper and bring to a boil
    4. Add the potatoes and 1/2 of the bacon to the boiling liquid.
    5. When heated through, transfer the potatoes to the serving dish.
    6. Garnish with remaining bacon and parsley.
